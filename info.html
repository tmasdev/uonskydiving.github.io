ABOUT US

Welcome to the University of Nottingham Skydiving Club! We are a supportive and inclusive club that offers students at the University of Nottingham the chance to gain their skydiving licence and jump out of a plane completely solo!

Our home dropzone is Skydive Langar, an old RAF base located 30 minutes from the university campus. Having completed 50,000 jumps in 2023, Skydive Langar is the UK’s biggest dropzone offering world‑class instructing and coaching for our members, and we’re proud to call it home.
